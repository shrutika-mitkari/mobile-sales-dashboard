# mobile-sales-dashboard

The Mobile Sales Dashboard is an interactive business intelligence project developed using Power BI and Excel to analyze mobile sales performance, 
customer behavior, transactions, and sales trends across multiple cities and brands.

This project helps business stakeholders monitor KPIs, compare sales performance over time, track monthly growth, 
and identify top-performing products and payment methods for better decision-making.


#  Business Problem

- Track overall mobile sales performance
- Monitor monthly and yearly sales growth
- Compare current sales with previous year performance
- Identify top-performing cities and brands
- Analyze customer purchasing behavior
- Monitor payment method trends
- Generate actionable business insights

# Business Insights

- Delhi generated the highest mobile sales revenue.
- Apple and Samsung were top-performing brands.
- UPI transactions contributed the highest payment share.
- Sales showed strong growth during certain quarters.
- Customer ratings were mostly categorized as “Good”.
- Monthly sales trends indicate seasonal fluctuations.

 
# Business Recommendations

- Increase marketing campaigns in low-performing cities.
- Promote high-performing brands during festive seasons.
- Improve customer experience to increase positive ratings.
- Optimize inventory based on monthly demand trends.
- Encourage digital payment methods for faster transactions.
